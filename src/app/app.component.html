 About Us Westrep Enterprises was founded in 1968 by C.K. Keeble and has been an operating manufacture's agency every since. In 1992 Dean Marriott took over Westrep to insure its future sucess. Dean with Mark Romey, handle all duties and requirements to effectively sell products for their client manufactures. Providing strong representation in today's ever changing market place.

Westrep has actively covered a territory which includes California, Nevada, Arizona, New Mexico, Utah, Colorado, Wyoming and Hawaii.

Today we operate from an office and warehouse located in a suburb of Los Angeles, near Pasadena. Westrep only sells the products for the manufacturers represented through qualified distributors/dealers. We have all the modern equipment and technology to maintain close contact with our manufacturers and mutual customers.
